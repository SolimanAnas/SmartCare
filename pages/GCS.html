Clinical Pearls
Document as GCS E_V_M = Total (e.g. GCS E2V3M4 = 9). Never document total alone.
️
GCS ≤8 = Intubation threshold — protect the airway. Consider RSI.
Reassess GCS every 15 min en route. A drop of 2+ points indicates deterioration.
Decorticate (M3) = cortical injury above the midbrain. Decerebrate (M2) = midbrain/brainstem involvement — worse prognosis.
️
Always check pupillary response alongside GCS — unequal pupils + low GCS = herniation until proven otherwise.
