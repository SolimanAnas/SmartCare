About SmartCare

The Platform

SmartCare is a clinical learning and revision platform — the engine underneath it isn't tied to any single course or certification track, so it can host whatever curriculum you point it at.

It's built for nurses, EMTs, paramedics, ER physicians, and nursing students who study between shifts, not just at a desk. Summaries, flashcards, quizzes, and scenario-based practice all work offline, straight from your phone.

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER SmartCare is intended purely for educational and clinical revision purposes. It is a study aid and must NOT be used as a primary clinical decision-support tool or direct reference for active patient care.

SmartCare does not replace institutional policies, local protocols, or professional clinical judgment. Always refer to authorized guidelines and standard institutional procedures.

Key Features

  • Progressive Web App: Install it like a native app on any phone, tablet, or desktop — it keeps working offline once cached.
  • Multiple Study Modes: Move between summaries, flashcards, quizzes, and scenario-based practice for the same material.
  • Instant Search: Find any topic across the whole curriculum as you type.
  • Progress Tracking: Quiz scores and completion are saved on your device, with optional Google sign-in to back them up and sync across devices.
